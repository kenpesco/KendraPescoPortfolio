Hello! I'm Kendra Pesco, a passionate 3D artist with a deep love for bringing ideas to life through the power of digital art. My journey into the world of 3D modeling and animation has been fueled by an unending curiosity and a desire to create immersive, visually captivating experiences. Whether it's character design, environment creation, or intricate animations, I strive to push the boundaries of my creativity with every project.

 

Currently, I'm honing my skills as a computer animation student, working on exciting projects that challenge me to grow and innovate. My work is not just about making things look good—it's about telling stories, evoking emotions, and creating art that resonates with people.

 

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of my favorite projects. If you’re interested in collaborating or just want to chat about all things 3D, don't hesitate to get in touch!
